## Two's Complement
**Find the two's complement of the binary number:**
   - 101010
   - 110011
   - 1001
To find the two’s complement, we:

Flip all bits (0 ↔ 1)
Add 1
1) 101010

Flip → 010101
Add 1 → 010110

Two’s complement: 010110

2) 110011

Flip → 001100
Add 1 → 001101

Two’s complement: 001101

3) 1001

Flip → 0110
Add 1 → 0111

Two’s complement: 0111
